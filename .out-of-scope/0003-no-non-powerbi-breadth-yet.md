# We do not chase non-Power-BI breadth yet

**Decision:** Power BI / the Microsoft semantic stack (PBIP, Fabric, semantic
models) is the sole entry point and current focus. We do not build for Tableau,
Looker, Qlik, or generic BI now — even though the Reflection *method* (metadata
in → topology → narrative → baseline → roadmap → enablement) is
technology-agnostic and would generalise.

**Why:**

- The wedge is sharpest in Power BI right now — PBIP made the estate
  machine-readable, which is what makes the Engine possible at all.
- The addressable market in Power BI alone, inside a single large agency, is
  more than enough to build the company on.
- Breadth dilutes the cross-estate pattern library — the moat compounds fastest
  when every estate we see is the same stack.

**Principle:** Focus beats breadth. Generalise only after the Power BI motion is
proven and repeatable.
