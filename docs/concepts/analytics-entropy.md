# Analytics Entropy

## Definition

Analytics entropy is the gradual degradation of enterprise analytics ecosystems over time.

Entropy emerges when:

- delivery pressure overrides governance
- semantic standards erode
- shortcuts accumulate
- unmanaged dependencies proliferate
- business logic fragments across reports

Analytics entropy is inevitable without active governance and architectural discipline.

---

## Core Insight

The majority of analytics technical debt is invisible until it reaches the semantic layer.

Power BI becomes the visible symptom of upstream disorder.

---

## Common Sources of Entropy

### Shadow IT

- Excel dimensions
- local CSV files
- unmanaged SharePoint lists
- manual mappings

---

### Semantic Drift

Metrics evolve differently across teams.

The same KPI develops:

- multiple definitions
- multiple filters
- multiple aggregation patterns

---

### Transformation Duplication

Business rules are reimplemented:

- in Power Query
- in SQL
- in DAX
- in spreadsheets
- inside visuals

---

### Gold Layer Erosion

Analysts bypass curated layers due to:

- delivery urgency
- missing dimensions
- incomplete semantic assets
- slow engineering cycles

---

## Organisational Drivers

Entropy is not caused by incompetence.

Entropy is caused by:

- delivery pressure
- insufficient platform maturity
- disconnected teams
- weak semantic governance
- lack of reusable data products
